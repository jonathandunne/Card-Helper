# Privacy Policy for Card Helper

**Last updated:** December 7, 2025

Card Helper ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application designed to help users view and manage credit card reward information. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to transparency regarding data handling.

---

## 1. No User Accounts

Card Helper does not require user accounts or authentication of any kind. Users are not required to sign up, log in, or provide identifying information to use the app.

---

## 2. No Data Collection

Card Helper does not collect, transmit, store, or retain any personal or user data on external servers.

- No personal information is collected
- No financial credentials are accessed
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising data is collected
- No data is transmitted off the device

---

## 3. Local-Only Data Storage

All data used by Card Helper is stored **locally on the user's device only** using on-device storage mechanisms such as **AsyncStorage**.

Local data may include:
- User preferences and settings
- Locally selected or entered card information

This data:
- Never leaves the device
- Is not accessible to the developer or third parties
- Can be removed at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Because Card Helper does not collect or transmit data off-device:

- No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties
- No third-party services receive user data

---

## 5. Data Security

Card Helper relies on the device’s operating system to protect locally stored data. While AsyncStorage does not encrypt data by default, the app does not store sensitive personal or financial credentials.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Card Helper does not knowingly collect any personal information and is safe for use by individuals under the age of 13, as no data is collected.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

Card Helper does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process user data.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If Card Helper’s data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date.
